FACE MIST

FACE MIST


DETAILS

    Get to know

    A gentle mist that locks makeup in place while absorbing excess oil on the skin's surface and controlling shine for an ultra-matte finish that lasts up to 8 hours.


    Feels

    Lightweight. Mattify. Suitable for all skin types.


    Colour

    Milky white.


    Volume

    80ml per bottle.


    Ingredients

    Aqua, Dimethicone/Vinyl Dimenthicone Crosspolymer, Glycerin, Propylene Glycol, Dimethicone, Trimethylsiloxysilicate, 1,2-Hexanediol, Butylene Glycol, Phenoxyethanol, Poloxamer 188, Poloxamer 338, C12-14 Sec-Pareth-7, Triethylene Glycol, SodiumBenzoate, Centella Asiatica Extract, Citric Acid, Xanthan Gum, Honey Extract, Citrullus Lanatus Fruit Extract, Garcinia Mangostana Fruit Extract, Cucumis Sativus Fruit Extract, Carica Papaya Fruit Extract.

HOW TO USE


    Spray generously and evenly onto the face, keeping eyes and mouth closed.

    Use before, during and/or after makeup application.

    Wear it alone to keep shine at bay on makeup-free days.
